About Me

Hi, I'm JahoKurdi, a passionate game developer and web enthusiast from Zakho, Duhok in the Kurdistan Region of Iraq.

I believe in the power of gaming and technology to bring communities together and create exciting new opportunities for Kurdish developers and players alike.
